    About Rankwell

    Rankwell answers “what should this site publish next?” by crawling real pages first, then planning keywords, a content calendar, and draft outlines from evidence on your machine — not from a SaaS black box or a one-shot chat prompt.

    • Site crawl — robots.txt, sitemap, and same-domain pages with a coverage report
    • Search themes — keyword candidates with intent, fit, difficulty, and question variants
    • Content calendar — a configurable 5–30 topic editorial plan mapped to formats and placements
    • Grounded drafts — page-aware outlines with evidence refs, visual plans, and automated QA

    Local-first and evidence-driven: crawls and projects stay on your machine. Codex AI is optional; a rule-based fallback is always available.

    Rankwell can use your existing Codex CLI login (~/.codex/auth.json). When Codex is not configured, rule-based fallback still produces themes, calendar items, and draft outlines.
